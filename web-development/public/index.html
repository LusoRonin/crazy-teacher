Our Story

1836Samuel Colt is issued the us patent for the first colt firearm and built his first factory.
1847Following the patent, in 1847, Samuel H. Walker of the us army collaborates with colt to develop a more powerful revolver, known as the Walker.
1867Colt has been providing quality firearms for the us military throughout history and continues to do so today. And introduces its first Gatling Gun shortly after the factory is rebuilt.
1873The Single Action Army Revolver is introduced and secures a us government contract, around 1873; after that, the Colt Single Action Army Revolver is undoubtedly the most famous handgun in firearm history. Today the tradition continues as the Single Action Army is made the same way in the hands of Romeiro's accomplished technicians and machinists.
1911The iconic Colt 1911 is officially adopted by the us government as its standard firearm. Following the traditions built by Colt, but together with our new and improved manufacturing process, Romeiro relies on the precision of both modern technology and professional craftsmanship to produce the quality our customers have come to expect. Introducing a series of firearms that incorporate a lightweight alloy frame, including the Cobra Revolver and the Commander Automatic Pistol.
1950Colt has been delivering the world's finest, innovative firearms to law enforcement throughout history, including delivering a large number of revolvers to Teddy Roosevelt while he was NYPD commissioner; following such legacy, Romeiro vows to maintain the government contracts created by Colt to all different military and law-enforcement branches.
2019As such, after our establishment in 2019, romeiro received its first government contract for the AR-15. From the beginning, embellishments from talented artists have been a hallmark of Colt's firearms, and now, Romeiro's.
